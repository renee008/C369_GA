About Us

Who We Are

Established in 2024, Renee Bank is a local bank providing various services such as banking, opening a savings account, offering credit cards to individuals.

Why Choose Us

Renee Bank uses mainly embedded finance and blockchain technology. Embedded Finance
Embedded finance involves integrating financial services into non-financial platforms, offering a seamless user experience. For Renee Bank, this means financial products such as payments, loans, and insurance are incorporated directly into the user experience, reducing the need for third-party services. This integration provides convenience and efficiency, enabling users to access a wide range of financial services within the same platform.
Blockchain Technology
Blockchain technology underpins the security and transparency of financial transactions at Renee Bank. By using a decentralized ledger, transactions are immutable and traceable, ensuring the integrity and trustworthiness of financial operations.

Our Latest Program

The Ultimate Reward Incentive Program. A program specially designed for low-income individuals to earn rewards as they spent. To apply for a credit card as long as they meet the following criteria:

  • Open a savings account
  • Monthly salary must be credited into this account
